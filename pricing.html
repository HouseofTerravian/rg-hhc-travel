FAQ

Common questions

Do both partners need an account? ▾
Monthly and Annual plans include both partners under one subscription. For daily purchases, either partner can unlock and share the exercise. We recommend doing the exercises together for maximum benefit.
How are exercises delivered? ▾
Exercises are delivered via SMS to your phone number, your web portal dashboard, and optionally via email. You choose your preferred delivery method in your account settings.
Can I switch programs after starting? ▾
Yes. Monthly and Annual subscribers can switch between any programs at any time. Your progress in your previous program is saved, so you can always return to where you left off.
How do vacation credits work? ▾
Every completed exercise earns vacation credits toward a couples trip through Happy Holiday Couples Travel. Monthly subscribers earn double credits and Annual subscribers earn triple. Credits can be redeemed at HHC.travel toward hotels, experiences, and flights.
Is this a replacement for therapy? ▾
No. Relationship Goals provides relationship exercises for personal growth and is not a substitute for licensed professional therapy or counseling. If you or your partner are experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact a licensed professional. See our Disclaimer for full context.
Can I cancel anytime? ▾
Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle. Individual exercise unlocks are non-refundable once accessed.
