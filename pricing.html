Questions

Common questions

What happens if we miss a day? ▾
You'll see it in your streak. Missed days are not penalized — but they are visible. The streak is honest. The goal is consistency, not perfection. If you miss a day, you pick it back up the next morning. Simple.
Do both partners need to unlock separately? ▾
One unlock covers both partners per day. You unlock together and complete the mission together. The mission is designed for two — you'll need each other to do it.
How do the local business coupons work? ▾
After completing your daily love mission, you unlock a coupon from a partner business in your area — restaurants, date experiences, wellness services, and more. As we grow our partner network, the coupon value will grow too. The goal is for completing your daily mission to pay for itself, and then some.
How do vacation credits work? ▾
Every completed mission earns vacation credits toward a couples trip through Happy Holiday Couples Travel (HHC.travel). 365-Day Pledge holders earn 3× credits. Credits accumulate in your account and can be applied toward qualifying bookings. The relationship you build today becomes the trip you take tomorrow.
What is Crystallize Lovers? ▾
Crystallize Lovers is the parent brand ecosystem that Relationship Goals lives within. If you're looking for a deeper membership experience — community, courses, coaching, events — that's the Crystallize Lovers home. Relationship Goals is the daily practice. Crystallize Lovers is the broader movement.
Are exercises refundable? ▾
Individual daily missions are non-refundable once unlocked — the content is delivered immediately upon purchase. See our full Refund Policy for details on the 365-Day Pledge and exceptional circumstances.
