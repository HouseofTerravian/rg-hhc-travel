Marriage Strengthening

A year-long daily commitment to deepening everything: intimacy, trust, shared meaning, and lasting connection. For couples who want to build something extraordinary together — one day at a time.

First 7 Days

Where the program begins

The Marriage Strengthening program builds from foundations to renewal across an entire year. Here is how the first week sets the tone.

Day 1
🏛️

The Foundation

"Strong marriages are built on knowing — not assuming — each other's truths."

Each partner answers three foundational questions: What brought you into this marriage? What do you believe marriage is for? What is one thing about your spouse that still surprises you? Share your answers without judgment. Let this be a reminder of who you chose.

⏱ 20–25 min 3 guided prompts included
Day 2
💌

Love Language Lab

"Love given in your language is effort. Love given in their language is mastery."

Each partner shares their top two love languages and gives a specific recent example of a moment when they felt loved — and a moment when they did not, despite good intentions. Then commit to one love language act this week.

⏱ 20 min 3 guided prompts included
Day 3
🔭

Shared Vision

"A marriage without a shared vision drifts. Tonight you set your coordinates."

Each partner describes their vision for the next five years of your marriage — what it looks and feels like, what you have built, who you have become. Find the overlap and draft three shared goals your marriage is moving toward together.

⏱ 25–30 min 3 guided prompts included
Day 4
🙏

Gratitude Ritual

"Gratitude, practiced daily, becomes the climate of your marriage."

Each partner shares five specific, genuine appreciations for the other — not general praise, but moments, qualities, and efforts they are truly grateful for. Then design a short gratitude ritual you can do every day: even one minute counts.

⏱ 20 min 3 guided prompts included
Day 5
🗺️

Adventure Contract

"Novelty is not a luxury in marriage — it is maintenance."

Together, plan one adventure for each of the next three months. It does not have to be expensive or elaborate — just new. A place you have never been. A meal you have never cooked. A conversation you have never had. Newness keeps the spark lit.

⏱ 20–25 min 3 guided prompts included
Day 6
🌩️

The Hard Conversation

"The conversations you avoid are the ones slowly eroding you."

Each partner names one topic that has been avoided — a real tension, concern, or unresolved issue. Then take turns speaking to that topic with honesty and without blame. The goal is not resolution tonight. The goal is that it gets said and heard.

⏱ 25–30 min 3 guided prompts included
Day 7
🕯️

The Renewal

"Commitment renewed with full knowing is the most powerful kind."

Each partner writes a short, personal renewal vow — not the ceremonial kind, but a true, present-day statement of who you are choosing and why. Read them to each other. Let this be a moment, not a performance.

⏱ 25–30 min 3 guided prompts included
