Reconnection Reset

For couples who've grown apart. You still love each other — but somewhere along the way, the closeness faded. This 30-day program rebuilds warmth, curiosity, and emotional closeness through daily rituals designed for two.

First 7 Days

Where the program begins

The Reconnection Reset starts by opening the door — one honest conversation at a time. Here is the first week.

Day 1
🚪

Open the Door

"Share something you've been holding back — even just a little."

Each partner takes turns sharing one thing they've been holding inside — something they haven't said out loud lately. The other partner listens without responding, fixing, or defending. Just listens.

⏱ 10–15 min 3 guided prompts included
Day 2
🙏

The Gratitude Exchange

"Say the things you feel but rarely say."

Each partner shares five specific things they genuinely appreciate about the other. Not general — specific. Specificity is what makes gratitude land.

⏱ 15–20 min 3 guided prompts included
Day 3
👂

The Listen

"Speak to be understood. Listen to understand — not to respond."

Partner A speaks for 5 uninterrupted minutes about how they've been feeling in the relationship. Partner B listens only — no fixing, no defending. Then switch. Reflect back what you heard.

⏱ 20–25 min 3 guided prompts included
Day 4
🌉

The Memory Bridge

"Go back to a moment when everything felt right between you."

Each partner shares one specific memory from your relationship — a moment when you felt most connected. Describe it in detail. Then identify what was present in that moment that you want more of now.

⏱ 15–20 min 3 guided prompts included
Day 5
🔍

The Honest Check-In

"Where are we, really?"

Both partners rate the relationship in four areas (1–10): Connection, Trust, Communication, and Fun. Share your number and the reason. Then pick one area to improve — and one small action each of you will take.

⏱ 20–25 min 4 guided prompts included
Day 6
🔭

The Vision

"What do you both want this relationship to look and feel like?"

Each partner describes their vision for the relationship — where do you see yourselves in one year? What does the relationship feel like at its best? Share your visions, then name the overlap out loud.

⏱ 20 min 3 guided prompts included
Day 7
💍

The Recommitment

"A simple, personal promise — made right now, to each other."

Each partner writes a short personal promise to the other. One genuine, specific commitment for the weeks ahead. Read them to each other. Keep them somewhere you'll both see.

⏱ 20–25 min 3 guided prompts included
