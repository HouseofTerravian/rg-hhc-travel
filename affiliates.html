Last updated: March 14, 2026

Transparency in Partnerships

Relationship Goals is committed to full transparency with our users. This Affiliate Disclosure explains how we work with partner brands, how commissions work, and how these relationships affect the platform experience.

Partner Coupons & Brand Rewards

When you complete a daily relationship exercise on Relationship Goals, you may unlock coupons and offers from third-party partner brands. These partnerships are commercial relationships — partner brands pay Relationship Goals a fee or commission in exchange for exposure to our user base through the rewards system.

This means:

  • Some coupons you receive may result in a commission or fee paid to Relationship Goals when you use them
  • Partner brands are selected based on relevance to couples and lifestyle — not solely on commercial terms
  • Our editorial and program content is developed independently and is not influenced by partner relationships

Travel Commissions (HHC Travel)

Vacation credits earned through Relationship Goals are redeemable through Happy Holiday Couples Travel (HHC.travel). Relationship Goals and Happy Holiday Couples are affiliated entities under the same brand family (Crystallize Lovers, a DBA of SlateRiver LLC, a New Mexico LLC). Travel bookings made through HHC.travel may generate revenue for the parent company.

Affiliate Referral Program

Relationship Goals operates an affiliate referral program. If you share your unique referral link and another user subscribes, you may earn additional vacation credits or other rewards. Specific terms of the referral program are outlined in your account dashboard.

Users who share Relationship Goals through affiliate or referral links are required to disclose this relationship when sharing on social media, blogs, or other public channels, consistent with FTC guidelines.

Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to external websites, products, or services. Some of these links may be affiliate links, meaning we may receive compensation if you click and make a purchase. This does not increase your cost. We only recommend partners we believe offer genuine value to our users.

FTC Compliance

This disclosure is provided in accordance with the Federal Trade Commission's guidelines on endorsements and testimonials (16 CFR Part 255) and affiliate marketing disclosures. We are committed to honesty and transparency in all commercial relationships.

No Editorial Influence

Affiliate and partnership relationships do not influence the development of our relationship exercise programs, program recommendations, or educational content. All content is developed based on evidence-based relationship principles, not commercial interests.
