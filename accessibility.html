ADA Accessibility Statement

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Our Commitment to Accessibility

Relationship Goals, operated by Crystallize Lovers, a DBA of SlateRiver LLC, a New Mexico LLC, is committed to ensuring that our platform is accessible to all users, including individuals with disabilities. We strive to provide an inclusive and welcoming experience for every couple who chooses to invest in their relationship with us.

Accessibility Standards

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with disabilities, including:

  • Visual impairments (blindness, low vision, color blindness)
  • Hearing impairments
  • Motor or physical disabilities
  • Cognitive disabilities

Accessibility Features

We have implemented or are working toward the following accessibility features:

  • Semantic HTML structure for screen reader compatibility
  • Descriptive alt text for images
  • Sufficient color contrast ratios for text legibility
  • Keyboard navigability for all interactive elements
  • Responsive design that works across devices and screen sizes
  • Clear, plain-language content throughout
  • ARIA labels on interactive components

SMS Accessibility

Our SMS delivery option ensures that couples can receive daily exercises without relying solely on visual web interfaces. For users with screen reader needs, our web portal exercises are formatted with clear heading structure and logical reading order.

Known Limitations

We are continuously working to improve accessibility. If you encounter any barriers while using our platform, we want to hear about it. Some areas of the platform may still be undergoing accessibility improvements, and we welcome your feedback to help us prioritize.

Reporting Accessibility Issues

If you experience difficulty accessing any part of the Relationship Goals platform, or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us:

  • Email: hello@rg.hhc.travel
  • Subject line: Accessibility Issue

Please include a description of the accessibility barrier you experienced, the page or feature involved, and the type of assistive technology you were using (if applicable). We will respond within 5 business days and work to provide an accessible alternative or resolve the issue in a timely manner.

Third-Party Content

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites (including partner brand coupon pages and Happy Holiday Couples Travel). We cannot guarantee the accessibility of third-party content, but we encourage our partners to maintain accessible standards.

Ongoing Efforts

Accessibility is an ongoing commitment. We conduct periodic reviews of our platform to identify and address accessibility issues. We welcome community feedback as a vital part of this process.
